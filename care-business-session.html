What the session is for

The goal is simple: help BNI members understand what it takes to get into care businesses, what tends to matter most, and what questions are worth asking before you jump in.

  • Practical insight from a Director of Care with real experience in the sector.
  • Space to ask about business models, operations, staff, and service quality.
  • Helpful if you are exploring partnerships, referrals, or a move into care.

What to send ahead of time

To make the 45 minutes useful, please include:

  • A couple of bullet points about what you want to get out of it.
  • Any specific questions you want answered.
  • Any other BNI members you think should be invited.

Who should come?

Any BNI member who wants to understand care businesses better, whether that is for referrals, partnerships, or simply to learn from someone who has been there.
